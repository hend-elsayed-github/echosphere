About Us
Saudi Ecosphere – Crusher Material Supply

We partner exclusively with engineering firms on mega-projects (malls, airports, highways) across Saudi Arabia. By specializing in bulk delivery, we fill orders that smaller suppliers can’t handle.

All materials are sourced from Al-Summan Quarry and delivered straight to your project location based on the required quantity and site.

We also provide sample delivery to your site upon request, so you can verify quality before confirming your order.
